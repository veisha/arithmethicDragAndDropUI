Game Mechanics

Welcome to the Drag and Drop Math Game! Here's how to play:

  • Drag numbers into the blank drop zones to form a valid equation.
  • The equation must equal the number displayed in the random number box.
  • If the equation is correct, you'll get a "Correct!" message, and a new equation will be generated.
  • If the equation is incorrect, you'll see "Incorrect. Try again!"
  • Use the Reset button to clear your answer or the Skip button to skip the question.
  • Click the Submit button to check your answer.
